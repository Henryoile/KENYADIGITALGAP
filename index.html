Skills Gap

The persistent mismatch between labor market demands and youth digital capabilities.

The Missing Digital Competencies

  • Basic Computer Operation & Software Fluency
  • Professional Digital Communication & Etiquette
  • Effective Online Research & Data Verification
  • Use of Productivity Tools (Spreadsheets, CRM, ERP)

Unequal Access Fuels the Gap.

The root cause lies in the deep disparities in access to quality digital education and training across Kenya.

  • **Inadequate Infrastructure:** Public schools and community centers lack computers, reliable power, and high-speed internet.
  • **Untrained Instructors:** A shortage of trained educators capable of teaching advanced, modern digital curricula.
  • **Socioeconomic Barriers:** High costs of personal devices, expensive data bundles, and limited exposure for youth in rural and low-income urban areas.

The Ripple Effect: Who is Affected?

The Youth

Stuck in cycles of underemployment or unemployment, missing out on the gig economy and entry-level formal sector jobs.

Employers & SMEs

Struggling to recruit digitally-literate staff, leading to reduced productivity, hiring delays, and limited capacity for digital transformation.

The Kenyan Economy

Slower local economic growth, increased dependency, and hampered national competitiveness in the global digital economy.
